## Slide 15

¿Mitigación de la injusticia?

**Figura:** Diagrama de flujo horizontal con 7 etapas del ciclo de vida de un modelo de ML, conectadas por flechas y con una flecha roja curva que retorna desde la última etapa ("Deployment & Integration") hacia la primera ("Viability Assessments"), indicando iteración. Etapas y tareas principales:
- Viability Assessments: 1. Cost-benefit analysis in comparison with status-quo, 2. Assessment of available resources, 3. …
- Problem Formulation: 1. Choice of predictive target, 2. Choice of predictive attributes, 3. …
- Data Collection: 1. Sampling, 2. Annotation (labeling), 3. Feature measurement, 4. Record linkage, 5. …
- Data Preprocessing: 1. Feature creation, 2. Feature Selection, 3. Data cleaning (Omission), 4. …
- Statistical Modeling: 1. Model selection, 2. Choice of objective function, 3. Optimization procedure, 4. …
- Testing & Validation: 1. Evaluation and Selection Metrics, 2. Train-test-validation splits, 3. …
- Deployment & Integration: 1. Human-machine interface, 2. Maintenance, 3. Oversight, 4. …

**Figura:** Debajo, un gráfico de barras titulado "% of papers vs stages", eje X = las mismas 7 etapas (Viability, Problem Formulation, Data Collection, Data Preprocessing, Statistical Modeling, Testing & Validation, Deployment & Integration), eje Y = "% of papers" (0 a 30). Tres series de barras por etapa: Problem Identification (verde), Measurement (azul/morado), Mitigation (amarillo). Valores aproximados: Viability ~1% (todas bajas); Problem Formulation ~5% Problem Identification, resto ~0; Data Collection ~6% Problem Identification, ~5% Measurement, ~3% Mitigation; Data Preprocessing ~3% Problem Identification, ~3% Measurement, ~6% Mitigation; Statistical Modeling ~5% Problem Identification, ~2% Measurement, ~31% Mitigation (barra dominante del gráfico); Testing & Validation ~2% Problem Identification, ~8% Measurement, ~5% Mitigation; Deployment & Integration ~6% Problem Identification, ~3% Measurement, ~3% Mitigation. Ilustra que la mayoría de los esfuerzos de mitigación de sesgo en la literatura se concentran en la etapa de modelado estadístico.
